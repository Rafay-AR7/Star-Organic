Sella Rice

Star Organics Sella Rice is a premium-quality parboiled rice that is rich in nutrients and delivers a light, fluffy texture with every grain. Perfect for making a variety of dishes such as biryanis, pilafs, and curries, it ensures a flavorful meal every time.

Benefits of Sella Rice:
  • High Nutrient Retention: The parboiling process retains essential vitamins and minerals, providing a healthier option.

  • Fluffy and Aromatic: Sella rice grains remain separate, soft, and aromatic, making it perfect for dishes like biryanis and pilafs.

  • Rich in Fiber: Sella rice provides a higher fiber content, contributing to digestive health and providing a fuller feel.

  • Versatile: Ideal for a variety of dishes, Sella rice pairs well with both spicy and mild cuisines.
